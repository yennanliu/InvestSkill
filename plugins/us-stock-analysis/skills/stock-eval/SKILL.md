# US Stock Evaluation

Perform comprehensive stock evaluation including:

## Analysis Components

1. **Company Overview**
   - Business model and competitive advantages
   - Market position and industry trends
   - Management quality and corporate governance

2. **Financial Health**
   - Revenue and earnings growth trends
   - Profit margins (gross, operating, net)
   - Balance sheet strength (debt, cash, assets)
   - Cash flow analysis (operating, free cash flow)

3. **Valuation Metrics**
   - P/E ratio (current and forward)
   - PEG ratio
   - Price-to-Book (P/B)
   - Price-to-Sales (P/S)
   - EV/EBITDA
   - Dividend yield and payout ratio

4. **Key Ratios**
   - Return on Equity (ROE)
   - Return on Assets (ROA)
   - Current ratio and Quick ratio
   - Debt-to-Equity ratio

5. **Comparison Analysis**
   - Peer comparison within industry
   - Historical valuation trends
   - Sector performance context

## Output Format

Provide clear, actionable insights with:
- Current valuation assessment (undervalued/fairly valued/overvalued)
- Key strengths and risks
- Investment thesis summary
- Price targets if applicable

Focus on data-driven analysis with clear reasoning.
